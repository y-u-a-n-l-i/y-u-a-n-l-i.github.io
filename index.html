Yuan Li

My name is Yuan Li(李渊 in chinese). I am a second-year master student in the State Key Laboratory of CAD&CG and advised by prof. Zhaopeng Cui.

I got my B.Eng degree in Computer Science from Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University in 2023. During my undergraduate research internship, I was honored to be advised by Prof. Shenlong Wang, UIUC Department of Computer Science.

I am interested in 3D scene modeling and editing, deep learning, and computer graphics.
